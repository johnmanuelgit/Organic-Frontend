A. Orders once placed & processed through themangobasket.com can be cancelled in 12hrs.

B. Estimated Delivery Time is the approximate 3-4 days to deliver the mango to the Customer. In the event of any delay or default in such delivery, Mango Basket will be in contact with the logistics partner and will share the detail with you.

C. If there is any shortfall in delivery or the mangoes such as they were damaged, pressed, squeezed or crushed, In such cases, you can send an email at hello@themangobasket.com or reach out to us at our social media handles and provide us required proof (image/video) so we can proceed with either refund or replacement.
