About Us

Our Story

Mango Basket is your way to goodness of Indian mangoes. Mango is considered the king of fruits and is one of the most widely produced fruit in India. It doesn’t matter which type of mango you love because at Mango Basket you will find all of them in one place. We are a brand focusing on delivering all types of authentic Indian residue free mangoes to you.

Fulfilling Mango Cravings

We connect mango lovers with their favourite type of mango

In whatever state or city in India you live, Mango Basket is committed to fulfil your mango cravings. Whether it is Hapus from Maharashtra, Himsagar from Kolkata or Langra from Uttar Pradesh — we deliver it all!

Empowering Mango Farmers

We Empower and Encourage Mango Farmers Not to Produce More, but Produce Better Mangoes

Mango is a fruit which offers a variety of health benefits, but if it is loaded with toxic chemicals, it may do more harm than good. At Mango Basket, we encourage and empower farmers to produce carbide and chemical-free mangoes — always focusing on quality over quantity.
